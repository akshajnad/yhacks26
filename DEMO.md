# Demo Flow

Goal:
Demonstrate how MedBill Agent detects billing issues and generates dispute actions.

---

## Step 1

Introduce problem:

Patients receive confusing medical bills and often do not know if they are correct.

---

## Step 2

User uploads:

- medical bill
- EOB

---

## Step 3

System parses documents.

Show loading:

Parsing bill  
Parsing EOB  
Comparing documents  

---

## Step 4

Audit screen appears.

Display:

3 issues detected  
Estimated savings: $1240  

Issues:

- duplicate charge
- patient responsibility mismatch
- denied claim

---

## Step 5

Show billing chain visualization.

Provider Charge → Insurance Processing → Patient Bill

Highlight mismatch.

---

## Step 6

Action center.

Generated assets:

- provider dispute email
- insurance appeal draft
- call script

---

## Step 7

Optional demo:

Start voice call assistant using ElevenLabs.

---

## Step 8

Final screen:

Case summary  
Recommended next step  
Estimated recoverable savings
